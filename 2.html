Path-finding Robot

Use of Atmel AtMega-8 with IR sensors to develop autonomous systems that seamlessly navigate through designated paths. This project focuses on building an autonomous robot that navigates a predefined path. Sensors like cameras will guide the robot, while a microcontroller translates this information into movement commands for the motors. The project entails designing the robot's structure, integrating sensors and motors, and developing control software. This journey offers valuable insights into robotics, electronics, and programming, with potential applications in automated tasks and educational platforms.
